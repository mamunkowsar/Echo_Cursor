Eligibility & team size
  • Open to everyone (students and professionals).
  • Solo or teams up to 4 members.
  • One submission per team.
What you can submit
  • Figma prototype or equivalent interactive link.
  • A short summary of your approach and decisions.
  • Optional: research notes, test results, or a short demo video.
Original work & assets
  • Use your own work or properly licensed assets.
  • Cite icon packs, illustrations, and fonts where applicable.
  • No plagiarism—violations lead to disqualification.
Code of conduct

Be respectful. No harassment, hate speech, or discrimination. Organizers may remove participants who violate this policy.

Score breakdown (example)

  • Accessibility: 15%
